# Domain: Social Survey (stub)

The social-research hat for general-population sociological work (distinct from social-service client surveys). Pair with the methodological core. Follows the contract in `../../DOMAINS.md`. **Flesh out when the first engagement is real.**

## 1. Scope
General sociological / social-research surveys of the public: attitudes, values, social conditions, civic and community studies, academic research.

## 2. Construct domains
public_attitudes, social_conditions, civic_engagement, values, overall_satisfaction, demographics.

## 3. Instruments & sources
- GSS (NORC) and major social-survey programs — tier B, with attribution.
- ACS / Census — tier A, for demographics and weighting targets.
- ARA's own instruments (incl. from *Социология управления*, Akhtyrsky 2022) — proprietary_ARA.

## 4. Compliance & legal layer
General research ethics: informed consent, voluntariness, confidentiality. GDPR-type rules for non-US respondents. IRB-style review where academic.

## 5. Benchmarks
Established social-survey time series (e.g., GSS) where comparable.

## 6. Sensitivities & population notes
General-population samples are strongly 3MC/multilingual; attitudinal items are the most culture-sensitive (see `shared/question-quality.md` section 5). Coverage and nonresponse are the dominant risks for population generalization.

## 7. Defaults
Mode: mixed (web/phone/face-to-face) by population reach. Sampling: probability where feasible, with weighting to population. Reporting: weighted estimates with uncertainty.
