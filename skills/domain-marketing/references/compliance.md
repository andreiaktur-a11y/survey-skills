# Marketing Compliance (domain-specific)

> **Status: stub.** Confirm current rules per channel and jurisdiction; this is a framework, not legal advice.

## Outreach consent / contactability
- **Email — CAN-SPAM:** accurate headers, opt-out, sender identification.
- **Calls & SMS — TCPA:** consent requirements for autodialed calls and texts; directly relevant to Retell voice/SMS fielding. Treat this as a gate before any automated outreach.
- **Non-US contacts — GDPR-type:** lawful basis, consent, data-handling, right to erasure.
- **Panels & incentives:** disclosure, no coercion, honor opt-outs.

## Data handling
- Minimize and protect customer PII; never put PII in survey URLs; aggregate for reporting.
