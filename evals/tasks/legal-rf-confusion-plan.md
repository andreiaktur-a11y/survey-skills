# Task: legal-rf-confusion-plan

**Path:** legal / RF. **Loads:** `domain-legal/SKILL.md` + its references + `shared/{question-quality,3mc-considerations,tse-framework,pricing-model}` and `survey-sampling`.

## Prompt
> A Russian client alleges that a competitor's sign is confusingly similar (сходство до степени смешения) to their registered mark on related goods. They need a consumer survey usable as evidence for Rospatent / the court. Produce a **survey plan**: relevant universe & screening, recognized format and question sequence, control approach, sample size and geographic spread, neutrality/double-blind safeguards, and the report structure. Methodology only — not legal advice.

## Pass criteria
1. **Universe** defined as prospective purchasers of the relevant goods/services, with screening logic.
2. **Format + sequence** named and operationalized: open (spontaneous source) → aided → control, i.e. the triangle rule; net confusion = test − control.
3. **Control done right for RF**: control **questions** required; a separate control **group/cell** treated as case-specific, *not* auto-applied (US-default not forced).
4. **Sample**: size in the 400–2000 band with rationale, socio-demographic + multi-region spread across the RF (not one city), randomness at the final stage.
5. **Safeguards**: double-blind administration, no leading items, full documentation / chain of custody.
6. **Report**: defensibility-first structure with methodology appendix; marked **expert signature required** and **confirm against current Rospatent-SIP practice**.
7. **No PHI**; no invented case-law thresholds presented as fixed cutoffs.
